Welcome to the beginners guide to magic circles! Here you’ll find everything you need to know to safely summon creatures from the beyond. But did you know? Summoning circles aren’t just for summoning demons, you can also summon sprites, spirits, angels and countless more otherworldly creatures to do your bidding. Most people summon nature sprites...

The first step in building your magic circle is figuring out what type of creature you want to summon so you know what the outer shape of your circle will be, and what material you need to use.
